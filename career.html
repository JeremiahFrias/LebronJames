NBA Career

LeBron James was drafted first overall in 2003 by the Cleveland Cavaliers. Over his career, he has played for the Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers, winning multiple championships.

  • Cleveland Cavaliers (2003–2010, 2014–2018) - Won 2016 championship
  • Miami Heat (2010–2014) – Won 2 championships
  • Los Angeles Lakers (2018–Present) – Won 2020 championship
